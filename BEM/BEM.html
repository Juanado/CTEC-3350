Gallery Component

The purpose of this component is to provide users with visuals from JavaJam. Images can not only be changed, but also added to this Gallery. Computer users can use the mouse to hover over the pictures to obtain a slightly bigger image of the picture they are currently over. Phone users, however, will need to click on the image to obtain a bigger picture.
